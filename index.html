Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Overview

Photo Collages processes your data locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to external servers by this app. The app does not collect usage statistics.

Photos Access

The app requests access to your photo library to let you select images and to save collages. You can grant limited or full access. If you enable automatic album saving, the app may create or update an album with the name you specify. You can change or revoke photo library access at any time in your device’s Settings.

Captions and Metadata

If you provide a caption, it can be embedded into the saved image’s metadata (EXIF/IPTC) on your device.

Sharing

When using the Share feature, only the selected collage is shared via apps such as WhatsApp, iMessage, Email, AirDrop, or Printing. Other photos in your library remain private.

Analytics and Tracking

This app does not include third-party analytics or tracking.
