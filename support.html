Photo Collages App – Support

Welcome to the support page for the Photo Collages app! Here you’ll find helpful information about using the app, customizing your collages, and troubleshooting common issues.

Getting Started

Choose a Layout

Start by selecting a layout from the Layout Gallery. You can:

  • Filter layouts by number of shapes
  • Show only favorite layouts
  • Start with a layout or begin by choosing your photos — both workflows are supported

Background Options

Backgrounds are only available for layouts where a background is visible. You can select:

  • One of the built-in background images included in the app
  • A photo from your own Photo Library

No additional background features are currently planned.

Preview & Editing

In the preview screen, you can:

  • Adjust overall composition
  • Set frame color and thickness
  • Add or edit a caption
  • Change the background (if supported by the selected layout)
  • Reorder or swap images between shapes

Adding and Editing Images

Add Photos

Tap any shape to choose an image from your Photo Library.

Zoom & Drag

Freely move and zoom images inside each shape for perfect alignment.

Automatic Shape Fill

Images always automatically fill the shape — no setting required.

Swap Images

Drag an image over another shape to swap them. If enabled in settings, zoom and alignment may reset after swapping.

Saving and Sharing

Save to Photo Library

Tap Save in the preview screen to store your collage.

Save to Albums

Save to an existing album or create a new one directly from within the app.

Share Directly

Use the Share button to send your collage via:

  • WhatsApp
  • iMessage
  • Email
  • AirDrop
  • Printing
  • All other iOS share options

Sharing works without saving first.

Limitations and Planned Features

Current Limitations

  • Shapes cannot be moved or resized
  • Custom layouts are not available

Planned Improvements

  • Custom layouts (future version)

FAQ

How do I save my collage to a specific album?

Tap Save and then choose an existing album or create a new one.

Can I swap images between shapes?

Yes. Drag an image over another shape to swap them.

Why can’t I select a background?

Backgrounds are only available for layouts where the background is visible.

Can I share my collage without saving it?

Yes. Tap Share to send it directly via your preferred apps.
