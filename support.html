FAQ

  • Q: How do I save my collage to an album?
    A: Use the "Save" button in the preview screen and select the album you want.
  • Q: Can I swap images between shapes?
    A: Yes! Drag and drop or use the swap feature in the preview.
  • Q: My image doesn’t fit the shape perfectly.
    A: Enable "Auto-fill shape" in Settings to automatically fill the shape.
