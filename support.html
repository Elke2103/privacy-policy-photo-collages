Photo Collages App – Support

Welcome to the support page for the Photo Collages app! Here you will find helpful information about using the app, configuring your collages, and troubleshooting common issues.

Getting Started

  • Select a Layout: Start by choosing a layout. You can filter layouts by the number of shapes they contain or show only your favorites.
  • Choose a Background: Optionally select a background image. Currently, this works best for layouts with only one shape.
  • Edit a Collage: In the Preview screen, you can adjust your collage, set a frame color and thickness, and add an optional caption.

Adding and Editing Images

  • Select an Image: Tap a shape to pick an image from your Photo Library. The image will appear inside the shape.
  • Zoom & Drag: Move and zoom your images freely within the shapes.
  • Auto-Fill Shape: When enabled in Settings, your image automatically fills the shape completely.
  • Swap Images: Drag an image across the border of another shape to swap them. Optionally, you can reset zoom and position to the initial state after swapping.

Saving Your Collages

  • Save to Photo Library: Use the "Save" button in the Preview screen to store your collage.
  • Create Albums: You can optionally create a new album and save collages directly into it.

Limitations and Planned Features

  • Moving or resizing shapes is currently not possible.
  • Creating custom layouts is planned for a future version.

FAQ

  • Q: How do I save my collage to an album?
    A: Use the "Save" button in the preview screen and select the album you want.
  • Q: Can I swap images between shapes?
    A: Yes! Drag and drop or use the swap feature in the preview.
  • Q: My image doesn’t fit the shape perfectly.
    A: Enable "Auto-fill shape" in Settings to automatically fill the shape completely.
